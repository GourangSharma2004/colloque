What's Overhyped
⚠ Overhyped
The Benchmark Parade — 93.9% SWE, AIME Scores, MMMU-Pro
Every lab dropped numbers. Every outlet ran them. Nobody asked who picked the baselines, which runs were cherry-picked, or what the production gap is. A jump from 90% to 93.9% on SWE-bench does not mean your codebase gets fixed. It means a lab tested something under conditions they chose.
Useful directionally. Useless as headlines.
⚠ Overhyped
Grok 4.3's 1M Token Context — Announced, Not Validated
Long context is genuinely valuable. But xAI published zero retrieval accuracy data at depth. The "lost in the middle" degradation problem is well-documented. A million-token window is a real engineering achievement. Whether it works reliably at 800K tokens in production is a different, unanswered question.
Feature confirmed. Capability unverified.
